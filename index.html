The Problem

Nigeria's Logistics Crisis

Fuel subsidy removal has pushed diesel prices to record highs. Businesses are paying more to move less.

⛽

Skyrocketing Fuel Costs

Diesel prices have tripled since subsidy removal, crushing logistics margins.

🏭

Carbon Emissions

Ageing diesel fleets pump millions of tonnes of CO2 into Nigerian cities every year.

🔧

High Maintenance Costs

Constant engine repairs keep traditional logistics businesses on thin margins.

📦

Unreliable Last-Mile Delivery

Lagos and Abuja businesses struggle to find affordable reliable delivery vehicles.

Our Solution

Electric. Efficient. Nigerian.

Young Giant deploys electric delivery vehicles across Nigeria's major cities — operated by local drivers, powered by clean energy, tracked in real time.

01

EV Fleet Deployment

Chinese-manufactured electric vans tailored for Nigerian road conditions and urban delivery routes.

02

Real-Time Tracking

Every vehicle is GPS-tracked. Businesses get live delivery updates from pickup to drop-off.

03

Solar Charging Hubs

Solar-powered charging stations solving the grid problem and cutting energy costs to near zero.

04

Driver Employment

Young Giant trains and employs Nigerian youth as certified EV operators creating sustainable jobs.

05

Business Logistics Platform

Businesses schedule pickups, track deliveries, and manage invoices through our digital platform.

06

VaaS Partnership Model
