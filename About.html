The Founder

From Warri
to Nigeria's Future.

Moses Reuben is a 24-year-old Nigerian entrepreneur from Warri, Delta State. He grew up watching Nigerian businesses struggle with the high cost of logistics, unreliable delivery services and the ever-rising price of diesel fuel.

When Nigeria's government removed fuel subsidies and tripled diesel prices overnight, Moses saw not just a crisis — but an opportunity. He asked one question: what if Nigerian businesses could deliver their products for a fraction of the cost, with zero emissions, using electric vehicles charged by the sun?

With nothing but a phone, a clear vision and extraordinary determination, Moses built Young Giant in just 10 days. No office. No investors. No co-founder. Just pure belief that Nigeria deserves better logistics.

Today Young Giant is in active discussions with BYD Nigeria, has been approved for the Startup Innovation Challenge, and has submitted applications to European-backed grant programmes — all built from scratch on a mobile phone.

This is just the beginning.
