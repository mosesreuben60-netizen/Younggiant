Why Young Giant.
Why Now.

1

First Mover Advantage

Young Giant is pioneering EV logistics in Nigeria before the market becomes crowded. First movers in African markets historically capture dominant market share that is very difficult to displace.

2

Government Backed Market

Nigeria's Federal Government removed EV import duties, approved ₦151 billion for EV deployment, and removed fuel subsidies — creating perfect storm conditions for EV logistics growth.

3

Proven Business Model

The VaaS logistics model is already working in Nigeria. ABC Logistics with CAWIN and eDryv with Dayun EVs have validated the concept. Young Giant is building on proven foundations.

4

Replicable Across Africa

Every African city facing high fuel costs and grid failures is a potential Young Giant market. The same model works in Ghana, Kenya, Tanzania and beyond — massive expansion potential.
